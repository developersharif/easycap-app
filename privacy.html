Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how EasyCap ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our screen recording software.

Information We Collect

1. Screen Recordings and Screenshots

- All recordings and screenshots are stored locally on your device
- We do not have access to your recordings or screenshots
- Content is never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share it

2. Application Data

- Application settings and preferences
- Keyboard shortcut configurations
- Software crash reports (with your consent)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the EasyCap software
  • Improve our software based on crash reports and usage patterns
  • Send important updates and notifications about the software
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your data:

  • All recordings are saved directly to your local device
  • No cloud storage or automatic uploading of content
  • Encrypted communication for crash reports and updates
  • Regular security audits of our software

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your application settings and data
  • Delete your application data and settings
  • Opt-out of crash reporting
  • Request information about your data
